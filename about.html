Our Story

Happy Design (The Responsible Partnership) is a premium design agency that designs beautiful websites for the best local businesses. We focus on quality, innovation, & speed. We keep things simple and affordable.

We know local, our founder James Murphy is a local business expert, having written community development plans published by the House of Lords, he works with local businesses to build strong, sustainable, resilient communities.

In 2021 in the height of lockdown he founded The Local Business Awards bringing much needed publicity to amazing local businesses and the communities they serve.
